The Legend of Zelda Series Overview

About the Series

The Legend of Zelda is a high-fantasy action-adventure video game series created by Nintendo. The series centers around Link, a courageous young hero, who embarks on various quests to rescue Princess Zelda and thwart the villainous plans of Ganon.

Iconic Games

  • The Legend of Zelda (1986)
  • The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past (1991)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time (1998)
  • The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker (2002)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess (2006)
  • The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (2017)
